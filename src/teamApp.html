TeamApp | Remote Team App

Built from scratch a responsive template for an app to collaborate and track project progress, designed to enhance customer support and engagement for businesses of all sizes. Aditionally I implemented cms in webflow to create a blog page.

I utilized Figma to design Logo, wireframes and develop the final prototype. For the website's development, I employed Webflow.



PROBLEM:

The client required a responsive website to showcase their application, which is designed to enhance customer support, improve engagement for businesses of all sizes, and facilitate collaboration and project tracking. Additionally, they needed a dedicated blog section to provide users with the latest updates on technology, design, and web development for businesses. The website also needed to feature user reviews, highlighting feedback from previous app users.

SOLUTION:

Created a fully responsive clean, modern design, ensuring optimal performance across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Used a color palette and typography that reflects the brand's identity while enhancing usability and user engagement.

Designed a dedicated section to showcase the app's key features, with clear descriptions and visual elements to demonstrate how the app enhances customer support, engagement, and project management.

Implemented a reviews and testimonials section where past users can leave feedback on the app’s performance and benefits. I Used a rating system and highlight key positive feedback to build trust and credibility with potential users.

Developed a blog page to regularly post articles on the latest trends in technology, design, and web development, catering to business owners and industry professionals. Ensured that the blog is easy to navigate with categories and tags, enabling users to find relevant articles quickly.

  • Responsive Website
  • Logo Creation
  • Contact Form
  • Reviews Section
  • Email News letter button
