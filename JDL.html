JDL Painting & Decorating

Built from scratch a responsive website designed for a company committed to delivering high-quality finishes that enhance the aesthetic appeal and durability of both residential and commercial properties.

I utilized Figma to design the wireframes and develop the final prototype. For the website's development, I employed CSS3, HTML5, JavaScript (ES6), Bootstrap, Node.js, and Visual Studio.



PROBLEM:

The client did not have a website for their painting and decorating business, relying solely on a logo and an email for communication. They required a professional website that would enable potential clients to contact them and schedule future bookings. Additionally, they needed a platform to effectively showcase their services and highlight their past work.

SOLUTION:

To address the client's needs, I designed and developed a professional, user-friendly website with the following key features:

Created a visually appealing and cohesive design that aligned with the client’s brand identity. Developed a color scheme, typography, and layout that reflected professionalism and trust.

Designed a dedicated section to highlight the client’s painting and decorating services with detailed descriptions. Implemented a portfolio/gallery page to showcase high-quality images of past projects, reinforcing credibility.

Integrated multiple contact options such as a contact form, live chat, phone number, and email to make customer inquiries seamless. Placed contact information in prominent areas, such as footer, and a dedicated "Contact Us" page.

Ensured the website was fully responsive across all devices (desktop, tablet, and mobile).



FEATURES

  • Dark & Light Mode
  • Automatic Slideshoes
  • WhatsApp Link
  • Google Maps View
  • Contact Form
